About the UK Polyamory Association

History

The UKPA was originally founded in 2022 by Giulia Smith, Jenny Wilson, Emily Payne, Grace Withers, Eunice Hung, Jennie Philips and Maxine Green. After actively supporting the community for 3 years the leadership group was experiencing burn-out and it was decided to pause operations until new organisers could be found. In late 2024 David Durant joined the organising committee in a coordinating role and in late 2025 we rebooted the Association with renewed energy!

Legal and Financial Structure

The UK Polyamory Association is a non-profit limited company registered at Companies House . Our Articles of Association commit us to using 100% of any money we collect on supporting the polyamorous community in this country.

We have committed to posting our financial records openly once a month on our blog as part of our "month notes". In addition our financial records will be checked monthly by our external auditor Ian Tustin .

Community

We are very must assisted in our efforts by contributions from the organisers of polyamorous communities throughout the UK via a regular online meetup. If you are in such a position and would like to be part of our leadership support network please get in touch!
