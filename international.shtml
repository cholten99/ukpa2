Polyamory organisations around the world

This is a starting point for people looking for polyamory (or wider ethical non-monogamy) organisations outside the UK. We list one organisation per country to keep things readable.

Please contact each organisation directly for the latest details. UKPA does not directly endorse any organisation listed. If you spot a broken link, or want to suggest a replacement for a country, please get in touch.
