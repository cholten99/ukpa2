What it's like

A vanilla, social space for both newcomers and experienced polyamorous people — no kink or play element. Each session has two parts: a discussion focused on a specific topic, and a peer support slot where you can bring any questions or challenges you're currently facing and get advice from the group.

Brief history

The group was started in September 2024 with the aim of creating a space to talk openly about different ideas, concepts and challenges in polyamory.

How to join

Events are listed on FetLife (account name: PolyamSupportGrp). Note that FetLife is an adult platform, but this is not a play, kink or sexual event — it simply uses FetLife as its event listing tool. You can also contact the organisers directly by email.

Community rules

  • Confidentiality – anything said in the space or group stays there. You may share something insightful, but with no identifiers of who said it.
  • No judgement – we respect that we all have different ways of looking at the same thing.
  • No cruising zone – don't come with the intention of picking up a partner.
  • Respect pronouns.
  • Respect the physical space – leave it as you found it.

Where it's based

We meet centrally in Birmingham, sometimes in the Jewellery Quarter or the Gay Village. The venues we choose are accessible with level entrances and accessible bathrooms. Find out more on our group page, or feel free to email directly for details.
