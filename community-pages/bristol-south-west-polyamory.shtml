What it’s like

For the monthly meet the group gathers in a local pub for friendly socialising and conversation. The first hour (2–3pm) is set aside for newcomers to ask questions about polyamory and get a friendly welcome from the group’s admins.

Regular members arrive from 3pm onwards. The community includes people who have been attending for over a decade as well as new members joining regularly.

The group also organises ad-hoc events such as picnics, coffee mornings, board game nights and walking socials. These are advertised in the Facebook group.

Flirting / dating vibe

This is a social group rather than a dating group. Members often form connections over time, but attending specifically to hit on people is discouraged and may lead to a permanent ban.

Members should ask consent before sending Facebook messages or friend requests.

Brief history

Bristol and South West Polyamory began in August 2014 with a monthly meetup on the third Saturday of each month.

The group has grown from around 12 members to more than 2300 within a friendly and diverse community.

How to join

Join the Facebook group and check the events tab for details of upcoming meetups.

Rules, privacy & expectations

Community rules and guidance about what to expect are available on the Facebook group.

Moderation & support

The organisers take reports of rule-breaking seriously and prioritise protecting the community. Issues are usually handled by quietly calling people in rather than publicly shaming them.
