Big Feelings ENM

A social and sharing circle for all ENM folx — a non-judgemental space created to foster vulnerability and build community in non-monogamy. Whether you practise open relationships, polyamory, or relationship anarchy; whether you're having the time of your life or struggling to make it work, this is a space where you can build a network of care.

What happens at the socials?

The evening has a clear structure that balances connection and sharing:

  1. Informal social hour — a chance to arrive, settle in, and meet other attendees.
  2. Small group sharing circles — you break into small groups where each person gets dedicated speaking time to share what's going on with their experience of non-monogamy. There is time to discuss what you've shared if you'd like — whether you want to talk things through with others or hear how other people relate to your experience. This is always optional and entirely at your discretion.
  3. Wider group debrief followed by another chance to socialise.

Brief history

Big Feelings ENM was founded in 2024 by Laurie, who wanted a space that could hold the complexity of feelings around the ENM experience — and to provide others with the same.

Ethos and agreements

This is a non-judgemental, supportive space. Full details of the ethos and agreements are on the Essential Info page.

Please note: attending the event to meet new partners, and any form of cruising, is absolutely not allowed and will result in removal from the space.

How to join

To attend an in-person social you must go through a vetting process and fill out a vetting form. This can be found on the event page.

Events are kept small and intimate so that people can be genuinely vulnerable about their ENM experience. The vetting process helps ensure everyone who attends understands what and who the space is for.

You can also follow @bigfeelingsENM on Instagram for announcements.
