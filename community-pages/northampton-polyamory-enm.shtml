About the group

Northampton Polyamory & ENM has been running regular meet-ups since 2015, building a warm community of over 300 members across Facebook and Fetlife. The group welcomes anyone interested in ethical non-monogamy — whether you're curious, newly exploring, or experienced.

What it's like

Meet-ups take place in a friendly Northampton town centre pub on the first Saturday of every month. The atmosphere is relaxed and sociable — great for catching up with people you know and meeting new faces.

This is a social group rather than a dating event. Dating and cruising are discouraged; anyone attending with that as their primary purpose may be asked to leave.

How to join

Join the private Facebook group or follow @NNPolyamory on Fetlife. Event details — including the exact pub location — are posted there to protect attendee privacy. If you have any questions before attending, post in the group, comment on the event, or send a direct message to the organisers.

The organiser on Facebook is Emily DF Payne. On Fetlife, contact @NNPolyamory.

Code of conduct

The safety and comfort of all attendees is the top priority. Anyone not following the code of conduct may be asked to leave or banned from future events. If something makes you feel unsafe or uncomfortable, please contact the organisers.

  • Always get consent before touching, hugging, touching someone's belongings, or asking personal or intimate questions. Anything other than an enthusiastic yes means no.
  • This is not a dating event. Whilst connections may develop naturally, please do not attend with that as your primary purpose.
  • Please do not attend if you are ill or showing symptoms of something contagious (cold, flu, COVID, etc.) — to protect immunocompromised members and their families.
  • Respect people's pronouns and identities.
  • Be mindful that the venue is a public space. Conversations and behaviour may be seen or heard by other pub patrons and staff.
  • Do not photograph other attendees.
  • Do not "out" anyone to people outside the group.
  • Do not cold-message people from the attendees list. If you have questions, post in the group or contact the organisers.
  • No genital profile pictures. Accounts with such images may not interact with the group or attend events — please update your profile picture first.
