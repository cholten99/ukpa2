What it’s like

We have people who’ve been coming over a decade, but we also have new people every single month. The meetup is structured so that everyone gets the opportunity to speak, but people can also come just to listen.

Flirting / dating vibe

Yorkshire O.P.E.N is a support group first and a community second. It’s common for people to make lifelong friends here, but it’s not a dating group. If you’re looking primarily to meet people to date, this isn’t the group for you.

Brief history

Yorkshire O.P.E.N started in February 2012, with a support group on the first Thursday of every month. During lockdown the meetup moved online and has stayed there — the organisers say the community is now more welcoming, safer and more accessible.

In-person socials still happen, but are organised ad-hoc (around once a month) and are often advertised only to existing members.

How to join

Start with the website and contact page above. When you attend your first event you automatically become a member. The community uses Discord, but it’s a semi-private server open only to members — you can be invited after attending.

Rules, privacy & expectations

Community rules and “what to expect” guidance are available on the Yorkshire O.P.E.N website .

Privacy information (including sharing information and photos at events) is covered in these rules.

Moderation & support

The organisers are very protective of the community and take all reports of rule-breaking seriously. Issues are handled by quietly calling in first, rather than publicly shaming people.
