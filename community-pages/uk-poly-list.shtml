What it’s like

The list has a mature tone and many participants have been thinking about and practising polyamory for a long time. Discussion tends to focus on real experiences, relationships and community life.

Traffic levels vary and the list is often fairly quiet, but long-time participants still value it as a place for thoughtful discussion.

Flirting / dating vibe

This is primarily a discussion list rather than a place for dating or personal ads.

Brief history

The UK-Poly mailing list formed from two earlier UK email lists some decades ago. For many years it operated using consensus decision-making and required an application to join.

It was formerly hosted by bi.org, although the list has always welcomed people of all sexualities.

Privacy

Membership of the list and identifying information about participants is treated as confidential.

Moderation

The list has a moderator who can step in when needed, though this has rarely been necessary.
