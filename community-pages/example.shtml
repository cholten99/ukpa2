About this group

OPEN (Organization for Polyamory and Ethical Non-monogamy) is a US-based organisation supporting people interested in polyamory and ethical non-monogamy. This Yorkshire group is part of a wider international network and offers a welcoming space to connect locally.

How to join

Join the main OPEN platform to connect with others, find events, and access resources. Local meetups and discussions are organised through the main hub and associated channels.
