About / what to expect

This group is a mix of long-time poly folks and people figuring things out. Conversations are typically social and supportive, with occasional structured discussion nights.

  • Come solo or with a partner/friend
  • No expectation to share personal details
  • It’s fine to be new, nervous, or just listening

Rules, privacy & safety

  • Privacy: Don’t out people. No screenshots from online spaces without consent.
  • Photos: Only with explicit consent. Default is no photos.
  • Consent: Respect boundaries; “no” is a full sentence.
  • Flirting/dating: Not the focus; keep it respectful and low-pressure.
  • Moderation: Contact mods via DM in the hub if there’s an issue.
