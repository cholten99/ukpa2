About this event

Join us for a picnic in Regent's Park to mark the Week of Visibility for Non-Monogamy— a chance to meet new people, enjoy the sunshine, and celebrate community with polyamorous and ethically non-monogamous folk from across the UK.

Whether you're new to non-monogamy or a seasoned veteran, come along, bring a blanket and some food, and say hello.
