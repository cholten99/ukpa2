Inclusion Statement

At TransformGov Talks, we are committed to creating spaces where everyone feels welcomed, valued, and respected.

We celebrate diversity in all its forms, and strive to be allies to LGBTQIA+ communities, people of all genders and gender identities, people with disabilities, neurodivergent people, people of every race, ethnicity, faith, and cultural background, and those from marginalised or under-represented groups.

We recognise that genuine inclusion requires ongoing effort. We will continue to listen, learn, and adapt so that our events and online spaces are safe, accessible, and supportive for all.
