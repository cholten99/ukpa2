What is Polyamory?

Polyamory is the practice of engaging in multiple consensual, loving, and committed relationships, with honesty and respect for all. For more information see our polyamory resources.

What is the UK Polyamory Association?

The UKPA is the United Kingdom's non-profit advocacy organisation for polyamorous people.

What is our mission?

We work to educate the public, support the broad and diverse polyamorous community in the UK and work towards social and legal change.
